Welcome to the Adventures of Thomas

Hi, my name is Thomas (as you may have gathered). I am currently doing a placement year in southern Germany, working for a software firm, where I am working on their sailing race-analytics software, which has been used, since 2011, for small and large regattas alike, including the legendary annual Kieler Woche (in Kiel). Next year, the software will also be deployed at the Olympics, off the beautiful coast of Marseilles.

Until then I will be working hard, exploring the surroundings, learning German and partying: this website aims to be a brief documentation of my travels and learnings. I hope you enjoy reading and feel free to contact me about any mistakes, suggestions or inaccuracies. Thanks :)
